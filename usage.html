Usage Guide

  1. Enter the name of the city you want to check the weather for in the search bar.

  2. Submit the search by clicking the "Search" button or pressing Enter on your keyboard.

  3. View the weather information for the specified city. You can see details such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

  4. If you want to check the weather for another city, simply repeat the process by entering a new city name in the search bar and clicking the "Search" button.
